How We Work

We are a close-knit team of colleagues and friends working on products we love. We believe great teams develop trust and confidence in each other through strong personal relationships.

What We Value

Our team is founded on common goals and values, and we strive to reflect these in our daily work.

  1. Lifelong learning & Mentoring

    Learning from and mentoring each other ensures we continuously grow stronger as individuals as well as a team.

  2. Craftsmanship

    Having true passion for our craft sets a standard to aim for as a team. We see the beauty in true craftsmanship.

  3. Honest Communication

    Productive partnerships are built on strong communication, where empathy and honesty is key.

  4. New technologies

    We don't have a go-to stack and are always on the hunt for new technologies to add to our utility belt.

  5. Zero HIERARCHY

    Confidence without attitude. Everyone in our team is a multidisciplinary problem solver who deserves to be heard.

  6. Humility & Curiosity

    Every day we seek to challenge ourselves to continually improve as a collective and as individuals.
